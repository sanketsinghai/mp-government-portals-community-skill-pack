# Domain Patterns & Common URL Formats — Madhya Pradesh Government

## State Government Domains

| Pattern | Organization | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| `*.mp.gov.in` | MP State Government departments | energy.mp.gov.in, tribal.mp.gov.in, health.mp.gov.in |
| `*.nic.in` | NIC-hosted district/department sites | bhopal.nic.in, mpvidhansabha.nic.in, mplokayukt.nic.in |
| `*.mponline.gov.in` | MPOnline-hosted services (TCS JV) | sarathi.mponline.gov.in, mpstdc.mponline.gov.in |
| `mphc.gov.in` | MP High Court system | mphc.gov.in, district.mphc.gov.in |
