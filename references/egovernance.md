## Key e-Governance Platforms Summary
- **MPOnline**: Single largest platform — 1700+ G2C services via TCS JV
- **MP e-Seva**: Unified portal for 56 departments
- **CM Helpline 181**: 24x7 grievance helpline
- **MP Bhulekh**: Digital land records for all villages
- **MP Tenders**: All government procurement
